What is Mobi?

Mobi is a ridesharing app that will allow people heading in the same direction the ability to connect with each other so they can rideshare on their commute.

How does Mobi work?

Pick a route

Simply enter your starting location and your destination. Then enter your desired pick up time and select the days you want to comute. That's right, Mobi helps plan your whole week!

Pick a commuter

Choose the person you want to travel with! Mobi allows you to select the person you want to commute with by offering everyone who is heading in that direction.

Chat before you ride

You can chat with your fellow commuter before the ride if you need to let them know something or just want to get to know them!

Why choose Mobi?

Build Relationships

At mobi we want to do more than provide an alternative transportation. We believe each and every commuter has a story, why not share it with the person next to you!

Improving an outdated system

Improving daily habits is just one step towards a more sustainable future. Reducing daily expenses will put more money in your pocket and also take cars off the roads helping the environment!
